About Gesture Play

Gesture Play is an innovative AI-powered gaming platform that lets users play games using hand gestures in real-time. Designed for fun, accessibility, and modern interaction, it combines computer vision and deep learning to bring you a unique, touchless gaming experience.

Whether you're challenging the computer in Rock Paper Scissors, solving puzzles in Tic Tac Toe, or testing logic in 2048, Gesture Play makes gaming interactive and futuristic!
