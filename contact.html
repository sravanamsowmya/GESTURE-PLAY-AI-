Frequently Asked Questions

1

What is GestureAI?

GestureAI is a revolutionary AI-powered hand gesture game controller that lets you play games using real-time hand movements detected by your webcam.

2

How do I set up GestureAI?

First, install the required dependencies (OpenCV and MediaPipe) using pip. Then, run the gesture detection script. Finally, open any game and start controlling it with your finger swipes!

3

Which games are compatible?

GestureAI works with games like rock paper Scissors and Tic-Tac-Toe and 2048 games.

4

Do I need special hardware?

No, you only need a standard webcam. Our AI processes your hand gestures in real-time using just your camera feed.

5

Is there a demo available?

Yes, you can visit our Demo page to see GestureAI in action and try out some basic gesture controls before integrating it into your games.

Need more help?

If you have further questions or encounter issues, please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team. supportgestureplay@gmail.com.
